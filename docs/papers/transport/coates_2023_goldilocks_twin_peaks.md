# Coates, Lovett & Gauger 2023: From Goldilocks To Twin Peaks

## Source

Alexandre R. Coates, Brendon W. Lovett, Erik M. Gauger, `From Goldilocks to twin peaks: multiple optimal regimes for quantum transport in disordered networks`, Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics 25, 10103-10112 (2023). DOI: `10.1039/D2CP04935J`.

## Physical Question

Is noise-assisted transport always controlled by one single optimal environmental coupling?

## Model

Physically modelled disordered transport networks with environmental coupling.

## Central Claim

Some networks can have more than one useful transport optimum. A simple single-peak Goldilocks picture can be incomplete.

## Limitations

The paper's peak structure requires full noise-scan curves. Our current broad campaign mostly stores best-point summaries.

## Relation with our lab

This paper tells us that storing only the best `gamma_phi/J` can hide structure. We need full efficiency-versus-dephasing curves for candidate networks.
