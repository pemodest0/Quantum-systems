# Engel Et Al. 2007: Wavelike Energy Transfer

## Source

Gregory S. Engel et al., `Evidence for wavelike energy transfer through quantum coherence in photosynthetic systems`, Nature 446, 782-786 (2007). DOI: `10.1038/nature05678`.

## Physical Question

Can coherent excitation dynamics be detected in a biological energy-transfer complex?

## Model / Experiment

Two-dimensional electronic spectroscopy of the Fenna-Matthews-Olson photosynthetic complex.

## Central Claim

Spectroscopic data show evidence for coherent, wavelike energy-transfer dynamics.

## Limitations

This is a spectroscopy experiment on a specific biological complex. Our effective graph model does not reproduce that experiment directly.

## Relation with our lab

This motivates the language of excitations and coherence, but it is not a direct benchmark for our generic graph simulations.

## Benchmark In Our Suite

`engel_2007.photosynthetic_wavelike_coherence_experimental_motivation`: not applicable to direct reproduction without microscopic complex parameters and spectroscopic observables.
