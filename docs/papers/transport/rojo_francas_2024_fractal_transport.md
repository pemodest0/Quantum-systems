# Rojo-Francas Et Al. 2024: Anomalous Quantum Transport In Fractal Lattices

## Source

Abel Rojo-Francas et al., `Anomalous quantum transport in fractal lattices`, Communications Physics 7, 259 (2024). DOI: `10.1038/s42005-024-01747-x`.

## Physical Question

How does non-standard geometry change quantum spreading?

## Model

Quantum transport on Sierpinski-type fractal lattices, with mean squared displacement used to classify spreading regimes.

## Central Claim

Fractal geometry can produce anomalous transport, including sub-diffusive or sub-ballistic behavior tied to spectral structure.

## Limitations

The current campaign does not include fractal graph families or exponent fits for anomalous spreading.
